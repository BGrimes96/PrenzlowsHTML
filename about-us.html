About Prenzlow Meats

Rooted in locker-room craftsmanship, guided by today’s best safety standards, and fueled by neighbors who’ve trusted us for generations.

In business for over 100 years

Old-World Skill. Modern Standards.

From the earliest days of icehouse lockers to today’s inspected facility, Prenzlow Meats has stood for straightforward quality: humane handling, careful trimming, and clean, freezer-ready packaging. We believe great meat starts with respect—for animals, for the craft, and for the families we serve.

Our promise is simple: be honest about what’s in the package, make it taste great, and treat every order like it’s going on our own table.
