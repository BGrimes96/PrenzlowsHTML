Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about ordering, pickup, storage, and our small-batch process.

Ordering & Pickup

How do I place an order?

Use ORDER HERE to choose locker packs or individual cuts. Not sure on portions? Add a note at checkout or contact us—we’ll help size it right.

When will my order be ready?

Most retail orders are ready within 1–2 business days. Custom cut lists (quarter/half beef, whole hog) take longer—timing is confirmed when we review your selections.

Do you accept special requests?

Yes. Tell us steak thickness, number-per-pack, grind preference, or sausage spice level. We’ll confirm what’s possible before we start.

How does pickup work?

We’ll notify you when your order is ready. Bring a cooler for longer drives. Our counter team reviews labels and counts with you at pickup.

Product & Preparation

What’s in your sausages?

Small-batch blends of meat, salt, and spices—no filler gimmicks. Seasonal batches vary; ask for today’s ingredient list.

Do you smoke your own bacon and jerky?

Yes—house smokehouse, balanced seasoning, and time/temperature profiles refined over decades.

How should I thaw meat safely?

Thaw in the refrigerator. For a faster option, use sealed-bag cold water; change the water every 30 minutes and cook immediately after.

What temperatures should I cook to?

Ground meats: 160°F. Whole cuts beef/pork: 145°F + 3-minute rest. Poultry: 165°F. Fish: 145°F (opaque, flakes easily).

Packaging, Storage & Allergens

How are items packaged?

Trimmed, labeled, and portioned for real-world freezers. We keep labels clear—cut, weight, and date—for easy rotation.

How long can I freeze meats?

Best quality guide: ground 3–4 months; steaks/roasts 6–12 months; bacon/sausage 1–2 months. Foods at 0°F remain safe indefinitely.

Do you list allergens?

Yes. Common allergens (e.g., dairy, wheat, soy) appear on labels when present. Ask the counter for current sausage ingredients.

Low-sodium or gluten-conscious options?

Choose simple, single-ingredient cuts most often. We can also suggest current small-batch sausages with lighter seasoning.

Custom Processing

Do you process quarter/half beef or whole hogs?

Absolutely. We’ll walk you through a cut list (steak thickness, roast vs. grind, sausage choices) and label everything for easy freezer rotation.

Can you help with sides or simple recipes?

Yes—ask at the counter. We keep quick side ideas and rub suggestions handy, and we’re happy to talk cooking methods.

We’ll help you pick cuts and portions that fit your household and your week.
