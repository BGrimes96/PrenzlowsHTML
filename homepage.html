Craft Butchery. Trusted Since 1920.

Prenzlow Meats is a heritage meat locker known for straight-forward quality and neighborly service. We source responsibly, prepare carefully, and stand behind every cut. Whether you’re stocking the freezer or planning a weekend cookout, our team brings a century of know-how to your table.

Our facility operates under rigorous inspection, and our processes reflect a commitment to freshness, cleanliness, and traceability. You get consistent product, fair pricing, and friendly help every step of the way.
