Our Mission

To honor a century of craft by delivering meat that’s honestly made, consistently labeled, and safe for every family we serve.

Pillars That Guide Every Cut

Integrity

Straight talk, clear labels, fair weights. We prepare every order like it’s going on our own table.

Food Safety

Clean-room handling, temperature logging, and traceability baked into our process.

Craft

Knife work, smoke, and seasoning developed over generations—never rushed, never gimmicked.

From Our Neighbors, For Our Neighbors

We prioritize responsible sourcing and relationships you can trace. When we say “local,” we mean names you can learn and people you can call.

Community Promise

We support school events, food drives, and neighborhood fundraisers. Good meat should do more than feed— it should belong to a place.

How That Mission Shows Up

Animal Care & Humane Handling

Respect at every step: transport timing, calm environments, and trained hands. Better treatment leads to better results—ethically and on the plate.

Radical Transparency

Ask what’s in the sausage, how we smoke our bacon, or why a cut is portioned a certain way—we’ll explain it plainly.

Sustainability that Makes Sense

Right-sized packaging for real freezers, waste reduction in cutting rooms, and equipment upgrades that save energy while improving safety.

Ready to Put Our Mission on Your Table?

Locker packs for weeknight sanity, or custom lists for the deep-freeze—either way, we’ll make it easy.
