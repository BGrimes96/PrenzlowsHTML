About Us & The Mission We’re On

Our Mission is to honor a century of craft by providing meats that are made with integrity, consistently labeled, and safe for every family we serve.

Prenzlow Meats, where tradition and flavor meet in every bite. Our processing plant is based in Snyder, Nebraska, and our retail location is in Waterloo, Nebraska. We carry on a legacy of crafting premium products using time-tested slaughter techniques passed down through generations. From signature sausages to specialty steaks, every batch is made with care and attention to detail. What sets us apart is responsible, local-first sourcing and radical transparency—so you can know exactly when and where your meat came from. We honor the past while delivering quality that fits today’s table.

Values That Guide Every Cut

Traceability

Every cut can be followed back to its exact farm and lot—so you always know what you’re serving.

Integrity

Straight talk, clear labels. We prepare every order like it’s going on our own table.

Food Safety

Clean-room handling and temperature logging baked into our process from intake to pickup.

Craft

Knife work, smoke, and seasoning developed over generations—never rushed, never gimmicked.

We Support Local, You Should Too.

We prioritize responsible sourcing and relationships you can trace.

  • “Local” means partners you can learn about and production you can verify.
  • Every cut traceable to the exact farm it came from—honesty you can check.
  • Stronger regional agriculture, even as we thoughtfully grow beyond our hometown.

How That Mission Shows Up

Animal Care & Humane Handling

Respect at every step: transport timing, calm environments, and trained hands. Better treatment leads to better results—ethically and on the plate.

Radical Transparency

Ask what’s in the sausage, how we smoke our bacon, or why a cut is portioned a certain way—we’ll explain it plainly.

Sustainability that Makes Sense

Right-sized packaging for real freezers, waste reduction in cutting rooms, and equipment upgrades that save energy while improving safety.
